10 Bok Choy Salad Recipes You'll Love.

1. **Asian-Inspired Bok Choy Salad**: Toss thinly sliced bok choy with shredded carrots, sliced bell peppers, chopped peanuts, and a tangy sesame soy dressing for a refreshing Asian twist.

2. **Bok Choy Slaw with Citrus Vinaigrette**: Combine thinly sliced bok choy with julienned apples, dried cranberries, and toasted almonds, then dress with a zesty citrus vinaigrette for a vibrant and crunchy slaw.

3. **Grilled Bok Choy Salad**: Grill halved bok choy until lightly charred, then drizzle with a miso dressing and garnish with toasted sesame seeds and green onions for a smoky and savory salad.

4. **Bok Choy and Mango Salad**: Mix chopped bok choy with diced mango, sliced avocado, red onion, and cilantro, then dress with a lime-ginger dressing for a tropical and refreshing salad.

5. **Spicy Bok Choy Salad**: Toss shredded bok choy with thinly sliced radishes, julienned cucumbers, and chopped cilantro, then drizzle with a spicy sriracha-lime dressing for a fiery and flavorful salad.

6. **Bok Choy and Quinoa Salad**: Combine cooked quinoa with chopped bok choy, diced tomatoes, crumbled feta cheese, and kalamata olives, then toss with a lemon-herb vinaigrette for a hearty and nutritious salad.

7. **Sesame Ginger Bok Choy Salad**: Mix shredded bok choy with sliced snow peas, edamame, shredded carrots, and sliced almonds, then dress with a sesame ginger vinaigrette for an Asian-inspired salad with a crunchy texture.

8. **Bok Choy and Tofu Salad**: Marinate sliced tofu in a soy-ginger marinade, then grill until golden brown and toss with chopped bok choy, sliced mushrooms, and bell peppers, and a peanut dressing for a protein-packed salad.

9. **Thai-Inspired Bok Choy Salad**: Combine shredded bok choy with sliced red cabbage, shredded carrots, chopped mint, and peanuts, then dress with a Thai-inspired peanut-lime dressing for a refreshing and flavorful salad.

10. **Bok Choy and Sesame Noodle Salad**: Toss cooked soba noodles with shredded bok choy, sliced cucumber, diced tofu, and a sesame soy dressing for a satisfying and nourishing salad that's perfect for lunch or dinner.
